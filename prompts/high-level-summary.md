## **Final Reference: *Cordimancy* (Complete Novel)**

Here is the complete, information-dense reference summary for your novel, *Cordimancy*.

### **üåé Overall Plot Synopsis**

The story follows **Toril**, the young heir of **Clan Kelun**, and **Malena**, his new bride. On their wedding night, Toril is forced by a political plot to **"challenge" his dying father, Hasha,** for the chieftainship. His father colludes with him to change the fight-to-the-death into a fight-to-cripple, and Toril wins by depriving his father of magic. He thus becomes the new chief, but must immediately leave Malena to attend a **war council** called by the manipulative **General Gorumim**. Gorumim, a 200-year-old **Sata** (who secretly *stole* Sata longevity through magic), is using a faked "osipi (half-life) invasion" as a pretext to start a war and consolidate power with his local ally, **Rovin**.

Toril, guided by the osipi **Oji**, discovers the lie and is the sole chief to vote "no," resulting in his arrest. While he is imprisoned, Gorumim's *real* forces (**bandits,** a **rakshasa,** and **oreni**) attack Toril's home of **Noemi**, slaughtering the population (including Hasha) and kidnapping the children. The attack is a **failed blood magic ritual** that required "total annihilation," but Malena's survival breaks the spell.

Toril escapes, finds Noemi destroyed, and resurrects the gravely wounded and traumatized Malena using a "great magic" (**kavro shilmar**) that **costs him his own magic** and **fertility**, leaving him physically weak. Now hunted by Gorumim and branded a traitor by Rovin, Toril and Malena‚Äîalong with Oji and the elderly couple **Shivi** and **Paka**‚Äîpursue Gorumim to rescue the children.

They evade an *ahu* ambush (led by Oji's father, **Luim**), but are forced to enter the **Blood Rift**, a magically cursed wasteland. Inside, they are attacked by **pishachas**, and **Paka is mortally wounded**. They escape the Rift, guided by Toril's magic **Staff of Kelun**, only to find Gorumim has **sacrificed four children** in a new ritual. After **Paka dies**, the surviving four (Toril, Malena, Oji, and Shivi) race to the capital, **Kikal Pilar**.

They are ambushed and **Toril and Shivi are captured**, allowing Malena to escape. Malena finds Gorumim torturing Toril and learns his final plan: to sacrifice the children at the **Harvest Festival reception** to break the Raja's "protective shells." Malena frees the mutilated Toril, and they confront Gorumim at the palace. As Gorumim kills his first victim, Toril **intercepts a magic bolt (meant for Malena) with his staff**, causing the magic to **recoil** and destroy the palace. The final reveal is that **Shivi used her magic to impersonate Tupa (Malena's sister)**, and *her* willful sacrifice ("heart magic") is what broke Gorumim's ritual. In the ruins, Toril and Malena, finally reconciled, reunite with Oji, Kinora, and Tupa.

### **üë• Character Dossiers**

#### **Main Characters**

* **Toril ur Hasha:** The new (but disputed) **Chief of Clan Kelun**.  
  * **Magic (Former):** Was a powerful **"lip"** (labimancer).  
  * **Magic (Current):** **Magic-less.** He sacrificed his unbound magic in the *kavro shilmar*. He then "unwove" his body's bound magic (sacrificing his **fertility**, or becoming **"stoneless"**) to resurrect Malena and Hika, leaving him physically weak.  
  * **True Name:** **"Helpless Seeker"**.  
  * **Status:** Alive. Badly tortured by Gorumim (hands "broken," mouth/tongue *burned* mute, one eye swollen shut). Reconciled with Malena.  
  * **Key Items:** **Staff of Kelun**. His **heartstone** is lost in the Blood Rift.  
* **Malena i Toril:** The **semanya of Noemi** (now destroyed).  
  * **Magic:** A **"heart"** (cordimancer), meaning no innate kindling. She has practical "book learning" in **herbalism (e.g., Bromavis)**.  
  * **Trauma:** A survivor of the Noemi massacre (implied **rape**).  
  * **Status:** Alive. Secretly **pregnant** from her assault. Has *wielded* the Staff of Kelun (Ch 55). She has found her sister **Tupa** and is reconciled with Toril.  
  * **Key Items:** Her **Antechild** (ocarina/daisy) was destroyed, but she learned Toril had *kept the shards*.

#### **Key Allies**

* **Oji (Oathizhi):** A **13-year-old "born osipi"** who is also a legendary **"aiki ahu"** (super-warrior).  
  * **Status:** Alive. He rescued Kinora and 4 other children during the palace collapse. He is unconscious from *aiki* trance.  
* **Shivi:** An elderly weaver and powerful **"hand"** (illusionist/healer).  
  * **Status:** **Deceased.** She **sacrificed her life** by using her magic to **impersonate Tupa**, taking her place as Gorumim's first blood sacrifice. Her "heart" magic (a willful sacrifice) broke Gorumim's spell.  
* **Paka:** Shivi's husband and a "cordimancer."  
  * **Status:** **Deceased.** Mortally wounded by a pishacha spear. Died and was burned on a pyre with 4 of the children, whom he was arranged to "cradle" like a "grandpa."  
* **Hika:** Toril's sheepdog. **Resurrected by Toril**. She is **guarding Cricket** in Two Forks with Corim.  
* **Corim:** The Kelun financial agent in Two Forks. He is loyal to Toril and is **protecting Cricket**.  
* **Tupa:** Malena's 11-year-old sister. **Alive.** She was a captive until the final battle and was rescued by Toril from the garden chaos.

  * **Status:** **Alive (presumably)**, but his political ally (Gorumim) is gone and his "osipi war" has been exposed as a lie.

#### **Other Characters**

* **Cricket:** The catatonic toddler. **Alive and safe** with Corim and Hika in Two Forks.

### **ü™Ñ World-Building & Magic (Cordimancy)**

* **Cordimancy ("Heart Magic"):** The novel's central theme. Revealed not to be a *lack* of magic, but the *true* magic of love, self-sacrifice, and forgiveness. It is the "heart" magic (a willful sacrifice) of **Shivi** (impersonating Tupa) that ultimately defeats Gorumim's blood magic, causing it to "recoil."  
* **Kavro Shilmar (Ordeal of Names):** The "great magic" Toril performed.  
  1. **Parable:** The "Bread and Ovens" (the "baking" *is* the process of "becoming" one's true name).  
  2. **Cost:** Toril sacrificed his unbound **"lip" magic** to *enter* the ordeal. He then sacrificed his **fertility** ("stoneless" / "Sata fork") to *use* the ordeal's power (resurrection).  
* **Gorumim's Magic:** He is *not* a true Sata. He is a **"lip"** (wizard) who used magic (likely blood magic) to **"get this white hair from sata throats,"** stealing their longevity.  
* **Gorumim's Grand Ritual:**  
  1. **The Noemi Ritual:** **FAILED**. Malena's resurrection broke the spell.  
  2. **The Kirte Fords Ritual:** **SUCCEEDED.** Gorumim **sacrificed four of the children** to "twist" the "tripwire" enchantment at the capital's border.  
  3. **The Palace Ritual:** **FAILED.** Gorumim killed his first victim (**Shivi**, disguised as Tupa), but her *willful sacrifice* ("heart magic") "tangled" his spell, causing it to recoil and destroy him.  
* **Staff of Kelun (Magic):**  
  1. **Guidance:** Shows Toril (and only Toril) cryptic glowing messages.  
  2. **Shared Vision:** Grants **supernatural vision** to *anyone* who is physically touching it (Ch 45).  
  3. **Conduit:** Capable of **intercepting a magical "plasma bolt"** (Ch 55), though it was Gorumim's *own* magic (tangled by Shivi) that caused the final implosion.
